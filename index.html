Privacy Policy – DayBee

2. What is this policy about?
This Privacy Policy explains which personal data the DayBee app processes, for what purposes it is used, to whom it may be transferred, and which rights you have as a user.

3. What data does DayBee process?
3.1 Account data (Supabase Auth)
When you register or log in, we process:
– Email address
– Internal user ID
– Authentication / session information

Purpose: Account access, account management, and synchronization of your data.
Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (performance of a contract).

3.2 Content you enter in the app (stored server-side)
Depending on usage, we process content such as:
– Daily plans / tasks / goals
– Notes / reflections / logs
– App settings you configure

Purpose: Providing core app functionality, in-app personalization, and synchronization across devices.
Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR.

3.3 AI processing (OpenAI)
If you use AI features, content (e.g. notes or planning text) may be transmitted to and processed by our AI service provider in order to generate suggestions or analyses.

Purpose: Generating personalized suggestions and evaluations.
Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR.

Note: Please do not enter sensitive information (e.g. health data) into free-text fields if you do not want it to be transmitted to the AI service for processing.

3.4 Technical data / logs
During operation, technical data may be processed, such as:
– Timestamps of requests, error codes
– Server-side log data for error analysis

Purpose: Security, stability, and troubleshooting.
Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest).

4. Recipients / service providers
We use the following service providers (data processors):
– Supabase: authentication, database and backend hosting (storage of account and app data)
– OpenAI: AI processing of content to generate suggestions
– PostHog: analytics

5. Data transfers to third countries
Depending on the provider’s location, data may be processed outside the EU/EEA (e.g. for AI services). In such cases, we ensure appropriate safeguards where required (e.g. standard contractual clauses).

6. Data retention
– Account and app data: until you delete your account or the data is no longer required to provide the service
– Logs / diagnostic data: typically retained for 30 days where required for error analysis

7. Your rights
You have the right to access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, data portability, objection, and withdrawal of consent.
Contact: daybeeapp-privacy@web.de
You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority.

8. Account deletion
You can delete your account directly in the app under:
Settings → Account → Delete account
Alternatively, you may request deletion via email at daybeeapp-privacy@web.de (please include the account email address).

9. Security
We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect data (e.g. encrypted transmission, access controls).

10. Changes
We may update this Privacy Policy. The current version is always available at:
https://daybeeapp.github.io/daybee-policy/
